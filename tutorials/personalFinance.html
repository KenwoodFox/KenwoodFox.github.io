A Personal Finance Guide for the Modern Person

By Joe Sedutto, September 2018

    Personal finance, a lot of people know right off what personal finance is, its your personal, finances, your money, and more importantly, how you mannage your own money. If you search "Personal Finance" into google, you get, thousands, millions, almost a billion results and counting artcicles and blogs and studies all with an underlying peice of advice "Save more than you Spend" well, there you go, personal finance seems easy, with such simple advice at our fingertips, it leads me to wonder why in this year, the average person spends 133% of their income.

    133% thats an extra 1/3d dollar spend for each dollar they earn, people overspend, people go into debt, why? In a study 2017, with research from a year or so earlier, CNBC reported that;
"Only 8 percent of [people polled] had a high level of knowledge, while about a quarter, or 24 percent, demonstrated just a basic understanding of how to manage their money. Yet 69 percent gave themselves high marks for financial know-how."
-Jessica Dickler, "Millennials aren't as smart about money as they think"  
if personal finance is so easy how can you have millions of americans going into debt like this. Obviously, we can figure this out, its clear this wisdom is old, unrefuted, and, by the 133% fact, the advice is ignored, so what can you do?

    People need another kind of article, a guide that does not just tell you what to do to not overspend, a guide that tells you why you are overspending, beacuse its clear that using conventional wisdom is not working, just like telling a smoker that smoking is bad, telling a person that debt is bad wont make them stop.

    Social Preasure. Social preasure is just what it sounds like, society putting preasure on you, you experince social preasure when you're waiting in line for coffee, you dont cut in front beacuse society tells you its bad, you wear clothes when you're in town beacuse society has preasured you to do that, and thats ok, social preasure is a double edged sword, able to keep a community together, but also able to make a communtiy do something, for example, when a group or community decide that drugs and using drugs are important, they may start to apply a social preasure to those outside them, to convince these people to try what they are doing. A humans, we are creatures of social tendancies, we want to fit in somewhere, even if its not the mainstream. So, what do social preasures have to do with finances? Advertising. Why do you buy what you buy? Why buy these expensive shoes over the plain cheaper ones? Advertising seeks to make you think something you want, like a fancy pair of shoes, is something you need, a purchace you need to fit into society, and that preasure is a great factor in making you pick a product, hire a service, or even vote for a political party.
